Welcome to the Intune Compliance Dashboard

Monitor and manage device compliance across your organization. This dashboard provides real-time visibility into Microsoft Intune managed devices, compliance policies, and security posture for JBKnowledge.

💻

Device Monitoring

View all managed devices across Windows, iOS, Android, and macOS. Track compliance state, last sync time, and enrollment status in real-time.

📋

Policy Compliance

Review compliance policies, conditional access rules, and app protection policies. Click any policy to see which devices are compliant or failing.

🚨

Alerts & Notifications

Configure alert rules for non-compliant devices, stale devices, and grace period expirations. Stay on top of compliance issues.

📈

Reports & Export

Export detailed CSV reports filtered by compliance state, grace period, or recent enrollments. Download and share with your team.

Alert Rules

Configure which alerts are active. Enabled alerts will show in the dashboard when conditions are met.

Non-Compliant Device Alert
Alert when any device falls into non-compliant state
Stale Device Alert (7+ days)
Alert when devices haven't synced in more than 7 days
Grace Period Expiring
Alert when devices are in enrollment grace period
Encryption Not Enabled
Alert when devices don't have encryption enabled
Stale Device Alert (30+ days)
Alert for devices that haven't synced in over 30 days (potential abandoned devices)
